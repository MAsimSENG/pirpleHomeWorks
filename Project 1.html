GTA Evolution

This website discusses the evolution of Grand Theft Auto (GTA).

What is GTA ??

     GTA or Grand Theft Auto is one of the most popular video games for adults. Throughout GTA, the player completes several missions which include:

  • Stealing and Robberies
  • Assassinations
  • Assaulting
  • Racing and hijacking vehicles

Personal Experience

     Personally, I spent quite long hours playing GTA and its versions and I had so much fun doing so. I like its structural details that make up a splendid experience for the player. The game play even of the early versions of GTA has fulfilled my with joy and excitement. What I really think is the main pron of GTA, is that each new series comes up with a revolutionary experience that is quite rare within video games in each time zone in which it had been released. I must admit that GTA contains so much violence and blood. But after all, I've always been obsessed by the various aspects and components of GTA such as:

  • The elite graphics of each new version
  • The well-constructed story
  • The unique game play

Graphics Evolution

Graphics Evolution

     In my opinion, in order to judge the graphics of each GTA, we have to reference the graphics technologies that the globe had reached in the specific time in which each GTA series was released. However, I will just compare between GTA series and other games that have been released in the same year. So that, I can judge how much good or bad is GTA.

Graphics Comparison
GTA I Other games released on the same year (1997)

     What I Think is there are some games that reached a better graphics than that of GTA I. But, as I always say "GTA is a unique experience" and this unique experience had made it out to the surface of the earth and collected much credit.
GTA II Other games released on the same year (1999)

     GTA 2 is another creative game which numberous of innovative missions ,but until then graphics has not said "I AM INCREDIBLE" yet.
GTA III Other games released on the same year (2001)

     GTA III made it through and has made glory in its most joyable form. I played almost all GTA Versions but this one is a very special to me. In GTA III, I was having perfect control, spectacular game play, and outstanding well-based story. The game has finally become a 3rd-person-view game with graphics what no one could see it and deny the incredibility of having such graphics in 2001.
GTA Vice City Other games released on the same year (2002)

     In 2013, graphics has become only one a few steps far from reality. GTA V was released and made a massive success. It became like a main need for any gamer of any gaming console. However, other splendid games were released in the same year with what's above any expectations. I feel that graphics of almost all the games has become equal in some way. Nevertheless, the game play is the one who give credit to the game when graphics can't give a decent sentence.
