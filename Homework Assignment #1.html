Pasta with Meat and Bechamel

  • Pasta Ingredients
    • Pasta
    • Mince Meat mix
    • Oil, Onion, Spices
  • Bechamel Sause Ingredients
    • 1/2 small glass oil
    • 1 small glass flour
    • 3+1/2 big glasses water
    • Salt
    • Black pepper
    • 3 table spoon milk powder

Preparation Method

  1. Roast the flour in the oil
  2. Mix the milk with the water
  3. Flow it slowly and stir them
  4. Add the salt and pepper
  5. If it needed more water, add a little more
  6. Put in the payer
  7. Then, another layer from the pasta
  8. Then, the pasta
  9. Then, the last layer from the bechamel sauce
  10. Put it in the oven for 10 min
  11. Roast it from top
  12. Finally, serve it hot
